Shipping and Delivery

We at Jivantrakshnam Organization are committed to delivering your orders with speed and care. Please find the details of our shipping and delivery policy below:

1. Shipping Locations

We currently ship our products to locations within India only. For international shipping, please contact our support team at [support@jivantrakshnam.org](mailto:support@jivantrakshnam.org).

2. Shipping Time

We strive to process and dispatch all orders within 1-3 business days after receiving the payment. Once your order is dispatched, the estimated delivery time will depend on your location and the shipping method selected.

3. Shipping Methods

We offer standard and express shipping options. You can choose the shipping method during the checkout process.

4. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges will be calculated based on the total weight of the order and the shipping address. The exact shipping charges will be displayed during the checkout process before making the payment.

5. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email or SMS. You can use this tracking number to monitor the status of your shipment.

6. Delayed or Lost Shipments

In the rare event of a delayed or lost shipment, please contact our support team immediately at [support@jivantrakshnam.org](mailto:support@jivantrakshnam.org). We will work diligently to resolve the issue and ensure you receive your order.

7. Damaged Items

If you receive any damaged items, please inform our support team within 48 hours of delivery. We may request images of the damaged items to facilitate the resolution process.

8. Change of Shipping Address

If you wish to change the shipping address after placing the order, please contact us at [support@jivantrakshnam.org](mailto:support@jivantrakshnam.org) as soon as possible. We will try our best to accommodate your request before the order is dispatched.
