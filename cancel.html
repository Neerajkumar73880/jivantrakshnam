Cancellation and Refund Policy

Thank you for choosing Jivantrakshnam Organization. We value your support and commitment to our cause. Please read our Cancellation and Refund Policy carefully before proceeding with any donations or payments.

1. Cancellation Policy

If you wish to cancel your donation or payment, please contact our support team at [support@jivantrakshnam.org](mailto:support@jivantrakshnam.org) within 24 hours of making the transaction. Once the cancellation request is received and verified, we will process the refund, if applicable, as per our Refund Policy below.

2. Refund Policy

Jivantrakshnam Organization follows a transparent and fair refund policy. Refunds will be issued under the following circumstances:

  • Unauthorized Transaction: If your account was charged for a transaction that you did not authorize, please contact us immediately for a full refund.
  • Failed Transactions: In case a transaction fails due to technical issues, and the amount gets deducted from your account, the refund will be initiated within 7 business days.
  • Double Payment: If you accidentally make a duplicate payment, please inform us at info@jivantrakshnam.org. We will verify the transaction and initiate the refund for the duplicate amount.
  • Other Circumstances: Refunds in other exceptional circumstances will be at the discretion of Jivantrakshnam Organization.

The refund will be processed using the original payment method. Please allow 7 to 14 business days for the refund to reflect in your account.

3. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our Cancellation and Refund Policy, please feel free to contact our support team at info@jivantrakshnam.org.
