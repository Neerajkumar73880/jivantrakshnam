About Us

Jivantrakshnam NGO is a beacon of hope, dedicated to transforming the lives of underprivileged communities. Our mission encompasses various initiatives, from providing education and healthcare to empowering women and supporting vulnerable animals. With your generous support, we aim to create lasting positive change. Join our fundraisers to make a significant impact on these deserving individuals and creatures.

Together, we can build brighter futures, offering opportunities for growth and happiness. Your contributions will be channeled efficiently to maximize their benefits. Let's unite in this noble endeavor, nurturing compassion and building a better world for all.

Our Causes

Cow Feeding

Join Jivantrakshnam organization's heartwarming cow feeding event, where we spread love and care to our bovine friends. Together, let's promote animal welfare and nurture a sense of compassion for these gentle creatures.

Gift Smile

Join our fundraiser for Jivantrakshnam NGO! Help us bring smiles to underprivileged children by contributing to gift distribution. Together, let's make a difference! Donate now and spread the love! 

Animal Health Care

Support our fundraiser for Jivantrakshnam NGO's animal health care initiative! Your donation will provide essential medical treatment, nourishment, and shelter for vulnerable animals.

Dog Feeding

Be a part of Jivantrakshnam organization's dog feeding event, as we show love and care to our furry companions. Join us in promoting animal welfare and nurturing a compassionate society.

Road Safety

Jivantrakshnam NGO promotes road safety through educational campaigns, advocating responsible driving, pedestrian safety, and adherence to traffic rules for safer streets and install tyres on dividers for minimum impact.

Food Distribution

Jivantrakshnam NGO ensures equitable food distribution, providing nourish to the underprivileged. Their dedicated efforts combat hunger by efficiently sourcing, storing, and distributing food.
